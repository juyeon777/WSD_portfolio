- **period:** 2024-02 ~ 2025-08 (paper published)
- A city-bus passenger project that started as a 2024 **industry-academia collaboration**.
- Conducted together with my advising professor and a senior (1st author), the research predicts city-bus passenger demand without alighting data, using transportation-card big data and reboarding counts (comparing Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM — LightGBM most stable).
- The results were published in the **Journal of the Korea Society of Computer and Information (JKSCI), Vol.30 No.8 (Aug 2025)** (co-author, 2nd author).
- **Methods (paper):** Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM (transportation-card big data, Python)

**My Role — My Part**

- During the industry-academia phase, visited the partner company (iTech) and joined meetings
- Collected and updated about one month of in-bus reboarding-count data for route No. 165 from the transportation-card big data system

- **period:** 2024-02 ~ 2025-08 (paper published)
- A city-bus passenger project that started as a 2024 **industry-academia collaboration**.
- It was later developed into **joint research** with my advising professor and a senior (1st author), studying a system that predicts city-bus passenger demand without alighting data, using transportation-card big data and reboarding counts (comparing Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM — LightGBM most stable).
- The results were published in the **Journal of the Korea Society of Computer and Information (JKSCI), Vol.30 No.8 (Aug 2025)**.
- **Role:** Co-author (2nd author)
- **Tech Stack:** Python, Random Forest, XGBoost, LightGBM, transportation-card big data, pandas
